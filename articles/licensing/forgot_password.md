# I forgot my password #

In order to change password which you don't remember open account menu:

<img src="../../images/resetpwd/signin.png"  style="width:500px"/>

Choose **Sign In** option.

<img src="../../images/resetpwd/iforgot.png"  style="width:500px"/>

You'll see login screen. Tap on **I forgot my password** link.

Specify recovery email:

<img src="../../images/resetpwd/recoveryemail.png"  style="width:500px"/>

And hit OK.

<img src="../../images/resetpwd/linkwassent.png"  style="width:500px"/> 

Check you mail box:

<img src="../../images/resetpwd/link.png"  style="width:640px"/>

Click on restore password **link**.

You will see the following screen:

<img src="../../images/resetpwd/pwdrestorelink.png" style="width:640px"/>


And finally you will get email with new password:

<img src="../../images/resetpwd/newpwd.png" style="width:640px" />
